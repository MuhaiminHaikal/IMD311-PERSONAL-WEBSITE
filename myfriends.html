MY FRIENDS

Friendship is a sweet responsibility, not an oppurtunity.

I discover that lots of things throughout life are beyond my control as I go through them. My parents, my genes, and the events that take place in the world around me are all beyond the control. Choosing a friends is something I can manage, and it has the power to either make my life more pleasant and richer or more stressful and depressing. Strong friendships are crucial for two major reasons. They improve the quality of life, to start. It might enhance our daily experiences to be able to share the lovely things in life with the people we care about. Secondly, our friends support us throughout our trying moments. Having friends at our sides during trying times may help even the most unbearably tough circumstances appear more bearable.

The most lovely thing about investing both energy and time in friendships is that we not just make our friends' lives better, but we also make our own! Friendships improve our lives overall, help us get through difficult situations, and add pleasure and enjoyment to our lives. Therefore, I love to make friends and I will keep the existence friendship with all school, university friends and close friends out there.
