I finished my Malaysian Certificate of Education here in 2020 while our world is still in the midst of the Covid-19 pandemic.

In the beginning, I was so excited to be a university student because it was my dream since childhood. I am grateful because the chosen course was the first option I applied for online. I enjoy studying here because I can gain a lot of knowledge and travel in other states. Now, I am in my last semester as a diploma student and maybe after this I will try to continue studying at the degree level.
